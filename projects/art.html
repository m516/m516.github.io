2023: Stable Diffusion-based Character Design

There's a lot of hype around AI-generated art, and the debate on its use is ongoing. As someone who can't draw to save my life, Stable Diffusion was a welcome addition to my 3D modelling and shading workflow.

This project was based on two reference photos generated by a variant of Stable Diffusion 1.4 called Mo-Di Diffusion, trained to emulate the Modern Disney style more accurately than the stock model weights. Thank you Nitrosocke for the weights!

I didn't spend too much time on texturing, shading, or grooming in hopes that I find time to complete the character some day. The textures were the reference images!

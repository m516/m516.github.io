2020

These are my first attempts at electronic music design, following my discovery of LMMS in early May (so don't expect greatness.)

Most instruments (including drums) are stock TripleOscillator presets or free MIDI soundfonts. The only custom audio effects were volume control.

2021

I gave composition a break in 2021 until my internship. This is when I discovered LMMS effects and instrument groups.

For the first time, human vocal vibrato patterns are emulated in leads by manually editing pitch automation in each note.

2022

Diversifying from house.

2023

My most recent experiments

Starting the transition from TripleOscillator and soundfonts to Zyn. LMMS doesn't support pitch automation with its native Zyn integration though, so leads are still TripleOscillators.
